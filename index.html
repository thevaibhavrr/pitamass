Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical project take to complete?

The timeline for each project varies depending on its scope and complexity. A simple branding project might take 2-3 weeks, while a comprehensive website redesign could take 8-12 weeks. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer customized pricing based on the specific needs of each project. We can work with fixed project fees, hourly rates, or retainer agreements depending on what works best for your business. We'll provide a detailed proposal with transparent pricing before starting any work.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer various support and maintenance packages to ensure your solutions continue to perform optimally. This includes technical support, content updates, performance monitoring, and regular improvements to keep your digital presence current and effective.

How involved do we need to be in the process?

We believe collaboration is key to success. While we handle the heavy lifting, we'll need your input at key stages such as discovery, strategy approval, and review milestones. The level of involvement can be tailored to your availability and preferences.
